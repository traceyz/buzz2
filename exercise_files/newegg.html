Bose OE2 headphones combine lifelike audio with lightweight comfort. They deliver the Bose sound you expect, but they are even smaller and lighter than the original on-ear headphones. You'll hear the full range of your favorite music, from deep lows to clear highs. The sleek, slim design looks great too—and comes in either black or white.

Big performance, small size

OE2 headphones feature an enhanced TriPort acoustic headphone structure, so you can experience big audio performance from these small earcups. TriPort technology delivers even the deep, low notes with clarity, letting you enjoy the full range of your music, movies or games.

The compact design offers a smaller, lighter and slimmer profile than the original on-ear headphones. Memory foam ear cushions conform to the shape of your ears, and the contoured headband sits evenly, without pressure points, on your head. They're so comfortable, you may forget you have them on—until someone tells you how good they look.

The detachable cable connects directly to one earcup, so there's less tangling than conventional Y-cables. And the headphones fold flat for storage in the protective carrying case.

 

  • Outstanding Bose Sound TriPort technology, available only from Bose, produces tonally balanced audio with deep low notes from lightweight, on-ear headphones. Bose acoustic equalization fine-tunes the frequency response of the headphones for exceptional audio performance.
  • Comfortable Fit Soft, cushioned earcups rest gently on your ears, providing a smooth, natural audio experience and a comfortable fit. Adjustable headband allows approximately three inches of adjustment in length. Sturdy design features soft cushion for added comfort.
  • Ease of Use Fold flat earcups rotate into headband for increased portability and easy storage. Detachable cable on a single earcup means less tangling and more convenient storage. Slim carrying case stores and protects headphones and accessories during travel.
  • Bose Authorized Dealer Newegg.com is an authorized Bose dealer, so that Bose product purchased from Newegg.com is genuine, factory-new and carries the Bose limited warranty. As an authorized dealer, Newegg.com has access to a wide range of Bose resources, enabling the kind of customer experience you'd expect from Bose, the most respected name in sound.

Rating: 5/5Wonderful Headphones

Pros: These headphones seem to be of high build quality, the fit and finish are better than others I have seen. The Plastics feel to be substantial and up to the task. The Headband has a nice padded section for comfort, and the ear cups have a soft memory foam under the leather (ette?) surface. This conforms fairly nicely for On-Ear headphones and blocks out a decent amount of outside noise.
As far as sound, these have a nice balanced sound, if lacking slightly on the very very high end (think like the crash of a high-hat) However these do not seem to suffer the Bose low end bloat their other products seem to be known for : i found it to have just the right amount of low end punch without being over saturated or boomy. They have balanced mids, fairly crisp highs and nice taught low end. Sounded great on a wide variety of music from Big band (Martin, Como, Sinatra) To Rock ( Beach Boys, Tom Petty, the Who) and funk (commodores, Midnight Star) from vinyl to 3k/bps Flac to low end Mp3

Cons: The only real con is the price.
While Not high compared to others, still a substantial price for non-noise cancelling headphones

Other Thoughts: the Price can be justified by the hours of use they will get,and compared to other headphones that offer similar sound quality the comfort seems to be much better for extended use due to the soft ear cups, soft headband and relatively light weight of the product
